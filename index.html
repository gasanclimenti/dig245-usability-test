(Un)usability

A test on www.espn.com for their user-friendless, organization, and accessibility.


Looking for Scores

Task: find the box scores from the most recent Carolina Panthers game.

Location: can be accessed on the NFL tab or the top of screen (harder to find if the Panthers lose and the font is colored in gray!)

Biggest plus: lots of different directions to find the same results.

Biggest challenge: the scores were often skipped over.

Time taken: slow

Task rating: unecessarily difficult

Result: successful, but timely


Listening to the Radio

Task: find and listen to live ESPN radio.

Location: first select “listen” on the top menu of the page, then select “listen live” or “ESPN radio”

Biggest plus: just a few clicks leads you to the right place

Biggest challenge: the ESPN radio page offers lots of different stations, some of which are not live

Time taken: quick

Task rating: efficient, simple, easy

Result: successful!


Creating a Fantasy Account

Task: Attempt to sign up for a fantasy sports leauge.

Location: click on “fantasy” then choose a specific sport (our tester selected hockey).

Biggest plus: joining a league is simple process and the page is well organized, with explantory buttons.

Biggest challenge: an ESPN account is necessary.

Time taken: quick

Task rating: easy, especially when fantasy leagues are advertised on the home page

Result: successful!


Checking out Little League

Task: find the scores from the most recent Little League World Series.

Location: Little League World Seires, among other sportsm can be found under the three white dots on the main bar at the top of the page

Biggest plus: ESPN provides scores for less-maintstream sports

Biggest challenge: with so many sports, Little League was hard to find; the scores were even more hidden

Time taken: slow

Task rating: tricky!

Result: successful, but timely


Watching a Video

Task: watch a related video and turn on captions.

Location: videos are posted throughout the home page, covering all different sports.

Biggest plus: videos are provided to showcase highlights in a format other than articles.

Biggest challenge: not all videos, like the one the tester found, provided close captioning (unaccessible).

Time taken: average

Task rating: unaccessible!

Result: somewhat successfull (fault of the site)
